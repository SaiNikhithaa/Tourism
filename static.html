Taj Mahal, India

The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is an ivory-white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Eiffel Tower, France

The Eiffel Tower in Paris is an iconic iron lattice structure built in 1889, attracting millions of visitors from around the globe each year.

Great Wall of China, China

The Great Wall of China is an ancient fortification built over centuries to protect Chinese states from invasions, stretching over 21,000 km.
